About Happy Cat Tavern

Happy Cat Tavern is a casual game that combines typing practice with tavern management simulation. Set in a world full of magic, you'll serve as a tavern owner, providing services to all kinds of cat customers.

Each visiting cat has their own unique preferences and stories. By interacting with them and providing services, you'll unlock more game content and storylines. Meanwhile, the typing challenge mechanism allows you to improve your typing skills while having fun.

Crafted with care by Courier Cat Studio, it's suitable for players of all ages, especially those who want to improve their typing skills in an enjoyable environment.
